Government Helplines

Official Emergency Numbers

These are government-run, free, and available 24/7

112
All Emergency Services
Single emergency number for police, fire, and ambulance. Works without network balance.
1091
Women Helpline
National Women Helpline. For any distress situation — harassment, abuse, violence.
100
Police
Local police emergency number. Available 24/7 across all states.
108
Ambulance
Emergency medical services. Free ambulance to the nearest hospital.
1930
Cyber Crime
National Cyber Crime Reporting Helpline. For online harassment, cyberstalking, and fraud.
1098
Child Helpline
For children in distress or danger. Also assists mothers and families.
